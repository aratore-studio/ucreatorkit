Our Tools

Each tool is designed to solve a specific problem that YouTube creators face daily:

Subtitle Extractor

Extract subtitles from any YouTube video in SRT, TXT, or VTT format. Supports multiple languages and auto-generated captions.

AI Video Summary

Get an AI-generated summary of any video, including key points and keywords. Perfect for research and content planning.

Thumbnail Downloader

Download video thumbnails in all available resolutions, from SD to MaxRes quality.

Tag Generator

Generate SEO-optimized tags, short-tail keywords, long-tail keywords, and hashtags for your videos.

Title CTR Analyzer

Score your video title's click-through potential with AI analysis, get improvement suggestions and CTR optimization tips.

Channel Analytics

View any channel's public statistics including subscriber count, video count, average views, and recent video performance.

Subtitle Translator

Translate video subtitles into any of 30 supported languages using AI, displayed side-by-side with the original text.

Chapter Generator

Automatically generate timestamped chapters for your videos using AI analysis. Copy-paste ready for YouTube descriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is UCreatorKit really free?

Yes, all 8 core tools are free to use with no account required. We support the service through advertising. Some premium features may be introduced in the future, but the core tools will remain accessible.

Do I need to create an account?

No. UCreatorKit requires no registration, login, or personal information. Just paste a YouTube URL and start using the tools immediately.

Is it safe to use?

Absolutely. We don't store your data, don't require personal information, and all communications are encrypted with HTTPS. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Why do some videos show errors?

Some videos may have subtitles disabled by the creator, or YouTube may temporarily rate-limit requests. If you encounter an error, try again after a few moments or try a different video.

Can I use the extracted subtitles commercially?

The subtitles belong to the original video creator. Please respect copyright and YouTube's Terms of Service. We recommend using extracted content for personal use, accessibility, or with the creator's permission.

How accurate are the AI features?

Our AI features use Anthropic's Claude, one of the most advanced language models available. While results are generally high quality, AI outputs should be reviewed before use, especially for translations and summaries of specialized content.
