Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Summary: UCreatorKit does not collect personal data, does not require user accounts, and does not sell any information. We process YouTube URLs you submit solely to provide tool results, and we do not store them.

1. Introduction

UCreatorKit ("we", "us", "our") operates the website ucreatorkit.vercel.app (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free YouTube creator tools, including the Subtitle Extractor, AI Video Summary, Thumbnail Downloader, Tag Generator, Title CTR Analyzer, Channel Analytics, Subtitle Translator, and Chapter Generator.

By using our Service, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use our tools, you may provide:

  • YouTube URLs — Video or channel URLs you paste into our tools for processing.
  • Text inputs — Topics for tag generation, titles for CTR analysis, and language preferences.

We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email address, or any other personal identifying information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Vercel) may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized in logs)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time of access
  • Referring website

This information is used solely for maintaining service performance and security.

3. How We Use Information

Information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Process your requests (extract subtitles, generate summaries, etc.)
  • Return results directly to your browser
  • Improve service reliability and performance

We do not:

  • Store YouTube URLs or processing results after your session
  • Build user profiles or track individual users
  • Sell, rent, or share any information with third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes

4. Third-Party Services

Our Service interacts with the following third-party services:

  • YouTube / Google — We access publicly available YouTube data (video metadata, subtitles, thumbnails) through YouTube's public interfaces to provide our tools.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — For AI-powered features (summaries, tag generation, title analysis, translations, chapters), we send transcript text to Anthropic's API. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy.
  • Vercel — Our hosting provider. Vercel may collect standard web server logs as described in their privacy policy.
  • Google AdSense — We may display advertisements through Google AdSense, which uses cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

5. Cookies

UCreatorKit uses minimal cookies:

  • Language preference — We store your selected UI language in localStorage (not a cookie) to remember your preference.
  • Advertising cookies — Google AdSense may set cookies for ad personalization. You can manage these through your browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

6. Data Retention

We do not maintain databases of user data. YouTube URLs and tool inputs are processed in real-time and are not stored after the response is sent to your browser. Server logs maintained by our hosting provider are automatically rotated and deleted according to their standard retention policies.

7. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • No persistent storage of user inputs or results
  • Secure API communications with third-party services

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is generally no personal information for us to provide, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Use our Service without providing any personal information
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
